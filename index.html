Speed

All of my web pages are fast. This means no unnecessary load time, quick and snappy transitions, and all while maintaining a pleasant user experience, and keeping good design.

Responsive

All of the projects I undertake, unless only temporary or just for fun, are responsive, meaning that they will work on any device. This is important to maintain a good user experience.

Design

As far as design goes, I stick to a relatively modern and flat design for my webpages. I add depth with the use of layers, parallax, and shadows. Colors are based on a pallete.
